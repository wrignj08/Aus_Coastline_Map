High Resolution National High Tide Coastline History

Australian Six Monthly High Tides (2015-2022)

About This Map

Explore how Australia's coastline has changed over time. Toggle between different time periods to compare high tide boundaries, or use the animation feature to watch coastal changes unfold from 2015 to 2022.

About This Dataset

This dataset provides high-resolution mapping of Australia's high tide coastline, derived from Sentinel-2 satellite imagery using the Normalised Difference Water Index. Created through collaboration between the Department of Primary Industries and Regional Development and Landgate.

Coverage

2015-2022 Time Period
6 Monthly Intervals
All Australia Geographic Extent
